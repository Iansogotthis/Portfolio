From Fast Food to Full Stack: How My Journey Shaped Me as a Web Developer

As I reflect on my professional journey, from managing bustling fast-food chains to pursuing a career in web development, I see a tapestry of experiences that have uniquely prepared me for success in this dynamic field.

Embracing Leadership and Problem-Solving

My tenure as a Shift Lead and Assistant Store Manager at Tri-City Foods and Cave Enterprises taught me invaluable lessons in leadership and problem-solving. Whether it was efficiently managing teams during hectic shifts or implementing innovative solutions to enhance operational efficiency, I honed my ability to lead and adapt in fast-paced environments.

Customer-Centric Mindset

Working directly with customers in the food service industry instilled in me a deep understanding of the importance of user experience. I learned to anticipate and address customer needs with empathy and efficiency, skills that are equally applicable in crafting intuitive and user-friendly web experiences.

Project Management and Adaptability

My roles as Assistant Restaurant Manager at Popeyes Corporate and Assistant Manager at Dunkin Donuts demanded exceptional organizational skills and adaptability. From overseeing complex projects to managing day-to-day operations, I thrived in environments that required quick thinking and effective decision-making.

Transitioning to Web Development

My passion for technology and continuous learning led me to pursue certifications in web development, including Full Stack Web Development from Lambda School and ongoing studies at App Academy. Through Codecademy, I've delved into HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React Fundamentals, laying the foundation for my journey as a junior web developer.

Looking Ahead

As I embark on this new chapter in my career, I bring with me a wealth of experiences that have shaped not only my professional skills but also my mindset and approach to problem-solving. With a foundation built on leadership, adaptability, and a customer-centric focus, I am eager to leverage my diverse background to create impactful web experiences that delight users and drive business success.
